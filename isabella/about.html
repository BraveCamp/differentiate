Meet the Team!

Hello! Three of us, Asha, Samaree, and Isabella worked on making a website for a week. Our overarching issue was media, and we broke that issue down into a smaller issue that we were all passionate about, which was stereotypes in the media. We went through the design thinking process, interviewed experts, and finally designed our website. Our great coaches at BraveCamp were able to help us make our website about stereotypes in the media come true. We want to educate teens what different stereotypes are in the media and how we can stop those stereotypes from spreading. We had so much fun working on this website and hope other teens will be able to use this information in their lives!
